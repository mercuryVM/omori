A unique experience for one player

Explore a strange world full of colorful friends and enemies. Browse the vibrant and mundane to discover a forgotten past. When the time comes, the path you choose will determine your destiny... and perhaps other people's destiny as well.

Player immersion and many hours of gameplay

Doesn't look like a big horror game, right? There are no scary monsters and existential horrors in the world of colorful dreams, but that's the point. Unable to control or face certain events in the real world, Omori conjures this fantastic headspace to escape, like a security blanket.

An impactful soundtrack

With over 100 songs spread over 22 hours of gameplay, OMORI leaves you stuck with their songs that perfectly match the mood of the moment.
